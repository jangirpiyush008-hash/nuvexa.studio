Curriculum

Six weeks,
loaded.

WEEK 01

Foundations of prompting

How models actually think. Tokens, temperature, context windows, and why your prompts fail. The 7 archetypes that cover 90% of real work.
  • Anatomy of a great prompt
  • System vs. user vs. assistant messages
  • Temperature, top-p, determinism
  • Lab: rewrite 10 bad prompts into great ones
WEEK 02

Claude, GPT & Gemini — when to use what

Strengths, quirks and sweet spots of each frontier model. Cost trade-offs, speed, reasoning, multi-model workflows.
  • Claude for long-form reasoning & code
  • GPT for agents & vision
  • Gemini for multimodal research
  • Lab: a research pipeline across all three
WEEK 03

AI for marketing & content

Blank page to publish-ready. Ad copy, landing pages, email sequences, SEO briefs, social engines — and the feedback loops that make them good.
  • Brand-voice prompts that don't sound like AI
  • AIDA/PAS/4Us frameworks prompted
  • SEO content briefs in 2 minutes
  • Lab: 30-day content calendar
WEEK 04

Image, video & audio generation

Midjourney, DALL·E, Stable Diffusion for static. Runway, Sora, Kling for motion. ElevenLabs for voice. Direct them like a creative director.
  • Prompt grammar for Midjourney v7
  • Motion-first prompting in Runway & Sora
  • Voice cloning & narration
  • Lab: produce a 30-second ad end-to-end
WEEK 05

Automations, agents & no-code

Connect AI to your real workflow. n8n, Make, Zapier, Claude agents, custom GPTs. Turn weekly tedium into 5-minute pipelines.
  • AI inbox triage agent
  • Claude Agents with MCP servers
  • CRM + AI feedback loops
  • Lab: ship one automation saving 5+ hrs/week
WEEK 06

Capstone — your AI operating system

Put it all together. Ship a working AI system for your role — launch kit, growth engine, content factory, or agent — with a demo and peer critique.
  • 1:1 mentor session
  • Live demo day
  • Certification & portfolio piece
  • Permanent alumni community

What do I get at the end?
