About

A studio
built to actually ship.

Nuvexa is a brand-new studio from Gurgaon, incubated inside Threebros Media. No 10-year portfolio, no inherited retainer contracts — just three founders obsessed with making the first wave of client work uncompromisingly, undeniably right. Web, mobile, creative, and automation — under one roof.

Origin

We started because the middle was broken.

Big agencies move like oil tankers — strategy decks, change-order forms, six-week "alignment" phases. Freelancers move like scooters — fast, but you're one flu away from a dead project.

Nuvexa is the third lane. Tight team. No handoffs. Founders in the pixels, founders in the code, founders on your Slack.

Our values

Four rules we
refuse to break.

01

Ship, don't stall

Weekly demos. Every project has a staging link by week two. Talk is cheap, diffs aren't.

02

Motion with meaning

Animation should reinforce the idea, not decorate it. Every scrub, reveal and transition earns its frame.

03

Fixed scope, fixed price

No mystery "strategy" fees. You get a sheet, a price, a date. We hit them, or we eat the overrun.

04

Say no, kindly

If we're not the right fit, we tell you and line up someone who is. Better for everyone than a bad build.

The team

A tiny crew of
misfits, makers & machines.

Small enough that there's no middle-management. Big enough that every discipline under our roof is owned by someone who lives and breathes it. No account managers. No handoffs. You talk to the person who builds.

✦

The Designer

Lives in Figma. Thinks in grids. Will redraw the same icon forty times until it's right. Moodboards at 2am.

◉

The Engineer

Ships before talking. Reads docs for fun. If your site is fast, it's their fault. Lighthouse 90+ or they don't sleep.

▲

The Motion Person

Keyframes things you didn't know needed keyframes. GSAP, Lottie, After Effects — all muscle memory. Apple is the benchmark.

The Strategist

☲

The Creative Lead

Obsessed with the first three seconds. Thumbnails, hooks, UGC. If it doesn't stop the scroll, it gets killed.

⚙

The Automator

Half engineer, half problem-eliminator. Turns your spreadsheet hell into a button. Talks fluent Claude & GPT.
